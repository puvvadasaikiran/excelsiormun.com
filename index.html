About EXCELSIOR MUN

Model United Nations

A Model United Nations (henceforth, abbreviated as MUN) is a recreation of the United Nations (UN Headquarters in New York, USA) proceedings, with students role-playing representatives of different countries in UN committees like the General Assembly, Security Council, International Atomic Energy Agency, Human Rights Council etc. MUN is a worldwide conference, where delegates from different parts of the World, Country come here and discuss on pressing Global issues which the world is facing. The purpose of a Model UN is for students to achieve a greater understanding not only of the UN, but also of global issues, through an interactive and enjoyable educational experience. It is an attempt to engage students with global challenges, and encourage them to think creatively with an international outlook to problem-solving, that attracts many of the brightest minds in the country. Previous UN Secretary-General Ban Ki-moon himself was once a MUNer. The success of an MUN lies in what it ultimately achieves. At the end of the 3-day session, our aim is to adopt workable resolutions in all three committee, which will be forwarded to the UNIC (United Nations Information Centre for India and Bhutan) office in Delhi.

Secretary General Letter

“I hate quotations. Tell me what you know.” - Ralph Waldo Emerson

Despite the irony of the quotation above by the poet R. W. Emerson, the importance and value drawn by it, is not what other people quote or think, but to introspect oneself and bring out the insights to contribute a broader sense of international harmony and understanding to a world that is faltering and needs help. While this might sound like a message to a particular few, in reality, it is our responsibility as one of 7 billion people inhabiting this world. Here, as EXCELSIOR '19 welcomes you all into bringing in this change of heart and mind, we are here to delve deeper into the untapped talent and potential of you being a harbinger to the changed and ideal world we hope for. Model United Nations is an opportunity for the to be leaders and challengers of the tragedies that befall the human race. EXCELSIOR MUN hopes to encourage healthy, balanced thinking to be applied in a diplomatic debate to bring out the most suitable solutions to first world problems. Changing the world with a legacy of rules and regulations is an easy task on paper, but in real life, your opinionated views and beliefs can stand a chance to be this world's Atlas, i.e, something that holds the world's weight. As you will come to see, the first world problems aren't the only problems and EXCELSIOR will rightly make this known to you by bringing in the problems here and now, and also problems that can arise. Co-operation and diplomacy will be instilled by forming alliances and truces to fight humanity's foes together as rightly said by Aesop, “United We Stand, Divided We Fall”. We at EXCELSIOR want to leave a lasting mark on your young and impressionable minds that, through diplomacy, speech and excellence in believing in oneself, we all can take this world to lofty heights as the Latin word "Excelsior" means. On a closing note, the quote “In a world of complex threats, our security and leadership depends on all elements of our power - including strong and principled diplomacy.” said by Barack Obama brings in the last note that when faced by unfavorable odds, our only hope for easing problems and lasting peace is not to use power, but through diplomacy, restrain it.

Regards,
Uday.
Secretary General,
EXCELSIOR MUN '19
